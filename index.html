Artificial Intelligence

The Role of Artificial Intelligence in Business Communication

Summary

Artificial intelligence has accelerated countless industries for over half a century and is constantly growing and maturing. The current global pandemic (COVID-19) has induced heavy reliance on the role of autonomous communication to solve business-related issues. Many tech companies are opting for intelligent software that can effectively evaluate and respond to high volumes of input. This website will provide a general overview of artificial intelligence, followed by some practical applications for AI’s use in business communication. Success stories will be displayed through example companies and business professional insights. Finally, several implications including the issues and inaccuracies of using AI will be discussed.

Background

  • AI’s origin derives from the mid twentieth century
  • Initial spending concerns caused a dip in AI’s development
  • Recently, AI has developed into a viable means of technology
  • Communication industry benefits greatly from AI and has been accelerated by COVID-19
